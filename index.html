panel | 9:30am-10:00am | ruby hall

Digital Detox: Using technology mindfully while maintaining personal and spiritual growth

This panel will delve into the concept of digital detox and its potential to enhance personal and spiritual growth. Panelists will share practical tips for finding balance, and creating space for a deeper connection with Allah.

keynote | 10:25am-10:45am | ruby hall

Connected And Centered: Finding Harmony Between Faith and Tech

The keynote will inspire attendees to find meaningful connections between their faith and their tech practices.

panel | 10:50am-11:30am | ruby hall

Harnessing the Digital Realm for Social Good

This panel will explore how Muslims can leverage digital platforms and technology to make an impact. Panelists will discuss successful initiatives and projects that use technology to solve social challenges and how Muslims can do the same.

panel | 12:15pm-12:40am | ruby hall

Managing your Money as a Muslim

This insightful panel session will delve into the intricacies of managing finances as a Muslim, exploring how to align financial decisions with Islamic principles and values. Join us as we discuss the concept of halal income, debt management, charitable giving, and strategies for financial success while upholding Islamic ethics.

workshop | 2:30pm-3:10pm | ruby hall

Personal Branding for Muslim Professionals

During this workshop, attendees will learn ways to effectively communicate their unique skills, values, and experiences to enhance their professional presence and build meaningful connections.

panel | 3:10pm-3:35pm | ruby hall

Faith At Work: Balancing Islamic Values and Professional Obligations

During this panel, experts will discuss ways Muslim professionals can navigate workplace situations while remaining true to their faith, fostering inclusion, and maintaining ethical practices.

workshop | 4:30pm-5:00pm | ruby hall

Unicorn Blueprint: Navigating the Journey from Idea to Billion-Dollar Startup

This panel will feature insights from successful entrepreneurs and experts to guide aspiring business leaders on the journey to building billion-dollar startups.
